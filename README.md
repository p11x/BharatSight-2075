# BharatSight 2075
### India Economic Intelligence Platform

An advanced Android app for economic forecasting and visualization of India's economy from 2026-2075.

## Features
- **31 Economic Sections** — Macro, Agriculture, Banking, Energy, Startups, Defence, Climate & more
- **45+ Chart Types** — Gradient area, neon candlestick, Sankey flow, radar, heatmaps & more
- **Official India Map** — GeoJSON-based neon political map with all states
- **AI Query Engine** — Gemini Nano powered on-device economic analysis
- **Live Market Data** — Real-time SENSEX, NIFTY, currency, commodity feeds
- **Economic Forecaster** — TFLite-powered 50-year GDP trajectory predictions
- **30 Map Styles** — Choropleth, growth pulse, digital connectivity & more

## Tech Stack
- **Kotlin** & **Jetpack Compose**
- **MVVM** Architecture
- **Hilt** (Dependency Injection)
- **Room** (Local Database)
- **TFLite** (Machine Learning)
- **Retrofit** (Networking)
- **Coroutines** & **Flow**
- **SceneView** (3D Rendering)

## Theme
Cyberpunk (Cyan/Orange) and Hologram (Blue/Purple) dual themes

## Build
- **Min SDK**: 26 (Android 8.0)
- **Target SDK**: 35 (Android 15)
- **Version**: 2.0.75
